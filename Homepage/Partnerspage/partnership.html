Who we are

Founded in 2010, Sky Smart Technology has grown from a small startup into a reputable leader in smart technology solutions. Over the years, we have expanded our expertise, partnered with industry leaders, and continuously innovated to meet evolving market needs.
Sky Smart Technology specializes in developing custom web applications, enterprise software, and intelligent systems that improve operational efficiency and business intelligence. Our capabilities extend to software automation, real-time monitoring systems, IoT-based solutions, and AI-powered platforms, delivering end-to-end support for digital transformation.
One of our standout innovations, the Andon alarm system, is widely implemented in garment factories. It boosts production line efficiency by immediately notifying responsible personnel of failures, enabling instant responses, and automatically logging accurate downtime data — all with minimal disruption to ongoing operations.
Whether it’s engineering hardware, software platforms, or web technologies, Sky Smart Technology continues to drive innovation, reliability, and excellence across every sector we serve.

Our History

Founded in 2010, Sky Smart Technology has grown from a small startup to a reputable leader in smart technology solutions. Over the years, we have expanded our expertise, partnered with industry leaders, and continuously innovated to meet evolving market needs.
Sky Technologies was established in Madampe in year 2004. Initially we created decorative LED light systems, projects support for engineering students,created some alarm systems and control systems for private and government sector institutes.In 2009 we have transferred our head office to Kegalle and had the opportunity to research and develop alarm systems for garment factories called “Andon Systems” in broader scale than earlier. Until now we have developed Andon Systems with various modern technologies like GSM,Wi-Fi and Radio Frequency Identification and also integrate with many systems for various customer requirements in order to optimize the accuracy, productivity and efficiency through automate the fault reporting and logging activities in a factory.From year 2010 onward, Sky Technologies have done the lighting up elephants for Perahera Festivals Island wide.Year 2010, it was the first time that lighted up all the elephants in Kandy "Dalanda Perahera" history.That was a golden milestone to us and glad to contribute for the historical event.
