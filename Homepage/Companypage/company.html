About

Founded in 2006, we’ve grown from LED lighting pioneers into a hub for electronics research, IoT-powered real-time monitoring, ANDON systems, and bespoke web applications. Serving South Asia and beyond, we deliver innovative solutions that exceed expectations.

Who we are

Since 2010, we have been at the forefront of industrial automation, initially focusing on ANDON systems for the readymade garment industry. Over the years, our vision and expertise have taken us beyond Sri Lanka’s borders—establishing a presence in Bangladesh, India, Vietnam, and Haiti. In parallel, we expanded our digital capabilities, crafting advanced Electronic designs and developing fully customized web applications tailored to the unique needs of enterprises and companies. Harnessing the power of IoT, we design and deploy real-time data capturing and monitoring systems that deliver instant insights and drive operational efficiency.
At our core, we continuously innovate in both electronics and software, ensuring that every solution we provide is reliable, forward-thinking, and aligned with our clients’ evolving goals.

Our History

Founded in 2006, our company began by specializing in LED lighting systems and steadily expanded its offerings over the years. Early on, we completed numerous projects for Telecommunication companies, demonstrating our commitment to quality and technical expertise. As demand grew, we broadened our scope, ultimately opening a dedicated electronics shop to serve both residential and commercial customers. Today, we continue to build on this foundation of innovation and reliable service.
